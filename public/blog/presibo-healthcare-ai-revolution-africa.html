Inside Presibo: How a Nigerian Healthtech Startup is Making Healthcare Accessible for All

SN
Salome Nnaji
Health Tech Journalist
📅 April 25, 2025
⏱️ 8 min read
🏷️ Innovation

In Nigeria, where over 97% of healthcare expenses are paid out-of-pocket, and a lack of access to quality medical services is still a harsh reality for millions, Presibo Health is emerging as a game-changer. This healthtech startup is redefining how people experience healthcare—by making it more affordable, accessible, and tech-enabled.

Co-founded by Jerry Nwobodo, a software engineer with a mission to solve healthcare gaps in Africa, alongside medical doctors Dr. Enny Aikodon and Dr. Derrick Udah, Presibo is powered by AI diagnostics, remote monitoring, digitized hardware testing, and an in-app chatbot that brings personalized healthcare to your fingertips. With a vision to serve underserved communities, the platform is removing barriers that prevent timely and effective care.

🚀 What Presibo Offers

  • 🩺 Telehealth Services
    Access to certified doctors anytime, anywhere, with flexible scheduling and virtual consultations that bring medical expertise directly to your home.
  • 🤖 AI-Powered Chatbot
    Intelligent assistant providing 24/7 support, answering health questions, and guiding users through their personalized health journey with smart recommendations.
  • 👴 Specialized Elder Care
    Dedicated health plans and regular check-ins designed specifically for elderly patients, ensuring continuous monitoring and age-appropriate care.
  • 💊 Prescription Delivery
    Medications delivered directly to your doorstep with same-day delivery options, especially beneficial for seniors and patients with mobility challenges.
  • ⌚ Wearables Integration
    Real-time health tracking through smart devices for comprehensive chronic condition management and preventive care insights.
  • 💰 Emergency Medical Financing
    Flexible installment plans and real-time loan approvals to manage medical emergencies without delay, removing financial barriers to urgent care.
  • 🏃 Personalized Wellness Programs
    Custom-tailored diet plans, fitness routines, and weight management programs designed to match each user's unique health profile and goals.
  • 📋 Electronic Medical Records
    Secure, encrypted patient data storage enabling seamless information sharing between healthcare providers and ensuring continuity of care.
  • 💳 Integrated Payment Solutions
    Streamlined payment processing for consultations, medications, and insurance claims, all managed within a single, secure platform.
  • 🔒 Advanced Data Privacy
    End-to-end encryption and full compliance with international data protection regulations, ensuring patient information remains completely secure.

👥 Meet the Visionary Team

Presibo is led by an exceptional team of medical professionals and tech innovators. Jerry Nwobodo serves as CEO, bringing software engineering expertise and entrepreneurial vision. Dr. Derrick Udah leads operations as Chief Operations Officer, while Dr. Enny Aikodon guides medical strategy as Chief Medical Officer. The leadership team also includes Oluwaseun Ayela as CTO, Dr. Asuquo Ekpenyong as Chief Innovation Officer, and Dr. Titilope Adedeji as Chief Programs Development Officer.

Together, they are combining deep clinical expertise with cutting-edge digital innovation to improve patient outcomes in real-time, creating solutions that address Africa's unique healthcare challenges.

🌍 The Bigger Picture: Transforming African Healthcare

"We believe cost should never be a barrier to life-saving healthcare. Presibo is more than an app—it's a lifeline for families across Nigeria and a beacon of hope for the entire continent."

With ambitious plans to expand across West Africa, Presibo is rapidly building a future where every African—regardless of their income level or geographic location—can access quality healthcare at the tap of a screen. The platform represents a fundamental shift toward democratized healthcare, leveraging technology to bridge the gap between medical expertise and patient need.

The company's impact extends beyond individual patient care. By collecting anonymized health data and insights, Presibo is contributing to broader public health understanding and helping inform policy decisions that can benefit entire communities.
