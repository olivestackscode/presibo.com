Inside Presibo: How a Nigerian Healthtech Startup is Making Healthcare Accessible for All

By Salome Nnaji | Published: April 25, 2025

In Nigeria, where over 97% of healthcare expenses are paid out-of-pocket, and a lack of access to quality medical services is still a harsh reality for millions, Presibo Health is emerging as a game-changer. This healthtech startup is redefining how people experience healthcare—by making it more affordable, accessible, and tech-enabled.

Founded by Jerry King Nwobodo, a tech entrepreneur with a mission to solve healthcare gaps in Africa, Presibo is powered by AI diagnostics, remote monitoring, digitized hardware testing, and an in-app chatbot that brings personalized healthcare to your fingertips. With a vision to serve underserved communities, the platform is removing barriers that prevent timely and effective care.

What Presibo Offers

  • Telehealth: Access to doctors anytime, anywhere, with flexible scheduling and virtual consultations.
  • AI Chatbot: Smart assistant offering round-the-clock support, answering questions, and guiding users through their health journey.
  • Elders Care: Special health plans and regular check-ins designed for elderly patients.
  • Prescription Delivery: Medications delivered to your doorstep, especially beneficial for seniors.
  • Wearables Integration: Real-time health tracking for chronic condition management.
  • Emergency Medical Financing: Flexible installment plans and real-time loan approvals to manage emergencies without delay.
  • Wellness Programs: Personalized diet, fitness, and weight loss plans tailored to each user’s profile.
  • Cloud-based Records: Secure patient data storage that enables seamless information sharing with doctors.
  • Integrated Payments: Users can easily pay for consultations, medications, and insurance within the app.
  • Data Privacy: End-to-end encryption and full compliance with data protection regulations.

Meet the Team

Presibo is led by Jerry King Nwobodo (Founder & CEO), alongside Dr. Derrick Udah (Chief Operations Officer) and Dr. Enny Aikodon (Chief Medical Officer). Together, they are combining clinical expertise and digital innovation to improve patient outcomes in real-time.

The Bigger Picture

“We believe cost should never be a barrier to life-saving healthcare,” says Jerry. “Presibo is more than an app—it’s a lifeline for families across Nigeria.”

With plans to expand across West Africa, Presibo is rapidly building a future where every African—no matter their income or location—can access quality care at the tap of a screen.
