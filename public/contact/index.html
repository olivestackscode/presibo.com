Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

How do I schedule a consultation with a doctor?

You can schedule a consultation by logging into your Presibo account and selecting "Find Doctors" from the menu. From there, you can browse available doctors and select a time that works for you. Alternatively, you can call our support line at +234 703 281 0862 for assistance.

Is the Presibo app free to download?

Yes, the Presibo app is completely free to download from both the Google Play Store and Apple App Store. While the app itself is free, some premium features and services may require a subscription or one-time payment.

How does the AI diagnosis work?

Our AI diagnosis system uses advanced machine learning algorithms to analyze your symptoms, medical history, and other relevant factors. It provides preliminary insights and recommendations, but always recommends consulting with a human doctor for final diagnosis and treatment. The AI is designed to assist, not replace, professional medical advice.

What areas do you serve?

Presibo currently serves all of Nigeria with our digital services. Our elder care services are primarily available in Lagos, but we're expanding to other major cities. Our mobile app is available nationwide for anyone with a smartphone and internet connection.
