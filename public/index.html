About Us

Presibo is a digital medical service that is helping patients get affordable and quality medical care by leveraging on Artificial Intelligence.

  • Continuous Care Services

    Ensure remote monitoring and personalized medical interventions for better patient outcomes and healthcare management.

  • Medical Cloud Services

    Help medical professionals, patients and medical facilities store, retrieve and share medical information for easy retrieval use.

  • Disease prevention, detection and diagnosis,

    Get health tips, exercise routines and AI generated health alerts for preventing diseases and living a healthier life
