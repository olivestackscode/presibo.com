What is PresiboPay4Me?

PresiboPay4Me is a compassionate initiative designed to help individuals and families access critical healthcare services by covering medical expenses, consultations, and subscriptions for those who cannot afford them.

Medical Bills

Pay for hospital treatments, medications, and emergency care to ensure timely medical support.

Consultation Bills

Cover doctor visits and virtual consultations for accessible healthcare services.

Subscriptions

Sponsor daily, weekly, or monthly Presibo health plans for continuous care access.

Who We Help

Children

Help kids receive the healthcare they deserve without financial barriers, ensuring a healthy future.

Adults

Support individuals managing chronic conditions or sudden illnesses with essential medical care.

Surgery Support

Fund life-saving or planned surgeries for patients who cannot afford critical procedures.

Ongoing Care

Provide continuous care through subscriptions to Presibo’s health plans for sustained wellness.
