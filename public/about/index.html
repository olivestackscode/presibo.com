About Presibo

Presibo is a digital medical service that is helping patients get fast, smart, stress-free, affordable and quality medical care by leveraging on Artificial Intelligence. Presibo uses AI and machine learning technology to bridge the gaps in Africa's health sector.

Our Mission

For Presibo AI innovation, our products are curated for improving healthcare in Africa. We are committed to making advanced healthcare technology accessible to every Nigerian, bridging the gap between cutting-edge medical innovation and local healthcare needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Use the search bar at the top of the "find doctors" page to type the service you need and your location (e.g., "I need a doctor in Lekki"). Press enter, and you will be shown a list of verified doctors nearby.

Presibo's Elders' Care Service is designed to provide specialized support for elderly individuals. Subscribers can access caregivers, remote monitoring devices, medication reminders, and health check-ins tailored to seniors' unique needs. To enroll, click the "Elders' Care" icon and complete the subscription form.

Presibo offers flexible subscription plans based on your healthcare needs. Plans include individual, family, and corporate packages with options for monthly, quarterly, and annual billing. Each plan provides access to our services, including remote monitoring, data storage, elders' care, and more. Visit the "Subscriptions" section on the app or website for detailed pricing and features.

Our remote monitoring service uses state-of-the-art devices to track vital health metrics such as blood pressure, heart rate, and oxygen levels in real time. Data is securely transmitted to your healthcare provider for continuous monitoring and timely interventions. Subscribers can access their health data and trends through their dashboard.

Presibo securely stores all patient data on a cloud-based platform using advanced encryption technologies. Users can access their data through the app or website, where it is presented in an easy-to-understand visual format with charts, graphs, and insights for better health management.

Presibo offers a range of remote care devices, including wearable fitness trackers, blood pressure monitors, glucometers, and pulse oximeters. These devices integrate seamlessly with the Presibo app to provide real-time updates and alerts for you and your healthcare provider.

To schedule an appointment with a doctor, log in to your account, click the "Book Appointment" icon, and select the type of consultation (e.g., virtual or in-person). Choose your preferred doctor, select an available time slot, and confirm your appointment. You will receive a notification with all the details.
